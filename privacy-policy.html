Welcome to GFXRhino! We care about your privacy and want you to feel comfortable using our services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your data.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

When you make a donation via PayPal, they collect your personal information, including your name and email address. PayPal securely handles this information and processes payments. We receive a notification from PayPal that includes your name and email to confirm the donation. However, we do not store, use, or share this information beyond the donation acknowledgment.

2. Non-Personal Information

We collect general, non-personal information such as how often our products are downloaded to help improve our website and services. This includes IP addresses, but it does not identify you personally.

If you make a donation through our Roblox game, we collect your Roblox username and display it in our public Discord channels. If you'd like to remove your Roblox username, please contact us on Discord.

How We Use Your Information

  • To Process Donations: We use your name and email address (via PayPal) or Roblox username to confirm donations.

How We Protect Your Information

We use secure payment gateways like PayPal to handle donations. We do not store payment information on our site. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee 100% security due to the nature of internet data transmission.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We share information with trusted third parties who assist us with our website and services, but they are required to maintain confidentiality.
